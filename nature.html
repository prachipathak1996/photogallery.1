Nature

Here's some information about Nature

Nature, in its broadest sense, encompasses the physical world, including all living and non-living things. It's a vast and intricate system that includes everything from the smallest atom to the largest galaxy. Nature provides us with essential resources and shapes our environment, influencing everything from the air we breathe to the food we eat.

An ecosystem is a complex, interconnected community where living organisms (biotic factors) interact with each other and their non-living environment (abiotic factors). These interactions maintain the flow of energy and nutrients within the system. Ecosystems can be natural, like forests or oceans, or artificial, like farms or aquariums.
